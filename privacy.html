Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2025  |  Last Updated: April 22, 2025

Lined & Loaded ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website linedandloadedstriping.com or contact us for services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, email address

  • Communications: Messages you send us through our website form or via text/call

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests and inquiries
  • Provide, schedule, and manage line striping services
  • Send you service updates, quotes, and follow-up communications

  • Comply with legal obligations

By submitting our contact form, you expressly consent to receive text messages (SMS) and phone calls from Lined & Loaded at the phone number you provide. This may include messages sent using an autodialer or prerecorded voice.

  • Consent is not a condition of any purchase
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and service status
  • To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any text message
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at (844) 955-1035

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist in operating our business (e.g., SMS/call platforms like Twilio)

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at linedloaded@gmail.com.

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to ad networks.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
